ML: Hot or Cold

 A tool that can classify water as hot or cold from the sound of pouring.

Choose an audio recording of water pouring, and see if the model guesses the temparature correctly!

(P.S.: For help, see this how-to guide to record and upload audio from an iPhone or a Windows 10 PC)

For the purposes of this ML model temparatures >= 175℉ are considered hot, and temparatures <= 75℉ are considered cold.
